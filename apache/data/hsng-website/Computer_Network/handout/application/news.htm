USENET NEWS
  • NEWS Server
    • Must have a newfeed from upstream site
      • news servers form a directed graph
      • Downstream news server periodically asks upstream server if any new news has arrived. If so, fetches the new arrived news.
      • Not every site gets all newsgroups
        • More than 500MB news per day
        • Some groups are too funky for administrators (alt.sex?)
    • Store each group of news on a single directory (e.g., news/comp/os/minix)
      • Subscribers of this news server then fetch from the same directory
    • News articles have the same format as RFC 822 email messages with additional headers
      • Path: list of nodes the message traversed to get from the poster to the recipient
      • Newsgroups: which newsgroups this message belongs to
      • Followup-To: since more than one newsgroups an article can be posted to, this header tells people where to post comments and reactions to that article
      • Distribution: how far to spread the posting: state/country codes, network, or "world"
      • Nntp-Posting-Host: analogous to the RFC 822 Sender header. It tells which machine actually posted the article
      • References: this article is a response to an earlier article and gives the ID of that article
      • Organization: tells what company, university, or agency the poster is affiliated with
      • Lines: length of the body
      • Subject: lines tie discussion threads together
      • Summary: summarize the follow-up article

  • NNTP - Network News Transfer Protocol
    • RFC 977
    • Design goals
      • To allow news articles to propagate from one machine to another over a reliable connection (TCP)
      • To allow users whose desktop computers canot receive news to read news remotely
    • To acguire recent articles, a client first establish a TCP connection with port 119 on one of its newsfeeds
      • When the connection is established, the client and server communicate using a sequence of commands and responses to ensure the client gets all the articles, but no duplicates.
        • Command Meaning
        LIST Give me a list of all newsgroups and articles you have
        NEWGROUPS date time Give me a list of newsgroups created after date/time
        GROUP grp Give me a list of all articles in grp
        NEWNEWS grps date time Give me a list of new articles in specified groups
        ARTICLE id Give me a specified article
        POST I have an article for you that was posted here
        IHAVE id I have article id. Do you want it.
        QUIT Terminate the session
    • Getting articles
      • News pull
        • Client initiates the connection to ask for news
      • News push
        • Newsfeed sends IHAVE to ask client if it wants
